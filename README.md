# 🇱🇰 Sri Lanka Tourism Analysis Dashboard (2000–2023)

An end-to-end data analysis project exploring 24 years of Sri Lanka's 
tourism trends using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

---

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes tourist arrivals, revenue, average stay duration, 
and country-wise tourism trends in Sri Lanka from 2000 to 2023 — 
including the impact of COVID-19 on the tourism industry.

---

##  Key Findings
- Sri Lanka tourism peaked in **2018 with 2.3M arrivals** and **$2.5B revenue**
- COVID-19 caused an **83% drop** in arrivals in 2021
- Despite low arrivals, **revenue per tourist hit an all-time high of $2,257** in 2021
- **India** is the largest source of tourists — 4.67M arrivals over 23 years
- High season generates **3x more revenue** than low season
